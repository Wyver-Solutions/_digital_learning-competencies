About the framework

An open source competency framework created by and for the digital learning industry. It sets the standard for professionals working in a broad range of roles, such as:

  • Design
  • Development
  • Consulting
  • Product design
  • Project management

The framework is based on 30 areas, each containing 3 levels:

  • Level 1: Foundation
  • Level 2: Practitioner
  • Level 3: Advanced

The levels are an indication of the amount of responsibility a person may have in this area.
